THE PRACTICE

A singular search for spatial balance.

I am an architect / urbanist working across different scale, bio-based materials, visual identity and resilience urban solutions. My practice combines architectural concept shaping, urban concept development, and urban data analysis. I am passionate about Urban studies.

Architectural Concepts

Developing spatial narratives that prioritize raw, local materials, connection with the natural landscape, and honest structural expression.

Object Design

Designing tactile furniture pieces and custom functional objects that emphasize raw joints, sculptural profiles, and grain textures.

Urban studies

Documenting structures and spaces in high-contrast analog formats, observing how shifting sunrays sculpt architectural forms over time.
